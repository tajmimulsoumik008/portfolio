About Me

Passionate about research, innovation, and technology in electronics and telecommunication engineering.

Hello! I'm Soumik, an aspiring researcher in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. My primary research interests include optics & photonics, signal processing, power electronics, wireless communication, and renewable energy technology.

Currently, I am working on a hand gesture-controlled wheelchair project and exploring signal processing techniques for power quality monitoring in renewable energy sources. I am highly interested in improving the efficiency of solar energy systems and enhancing renewable energy integration.

My long-term goal is to pursue an MSc and PhD in the USA or Canada with full funding and contribute to academia as a university lecturer in Bangladesh. To achieve this, I am actively working on research and publishing papers in reputed journals.
